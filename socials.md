## FAQ Snippets (for replies/engagement)

**Q: What happens to my deposit if I lose games?**
A: Your deposit stays in the vault earning yield. Only FP (derived from your deposit) is wagered. If you lose, that FP burns but your USDC remains.

**Q: Can I withdraw anytime?**
A: Yes, but withdraw >50% between epochs and your time multiplier resets to 0. This prevents deposit timing manipulation.

**Q: How are rewards calculated?**
A: At epoch end, winning faction's total USDC reward pool is split proportionally based on each player's FP contribution during the epoch.

**Q: What's the minimum deposit?**
A: No minimum, but multipliers use smooth piecewise curves. Deposits get peak FP boost at $1K (2.45x), then gradually decrease back to 1.0x at $10K. Similarly, time peaks at 35 days, drops back to 1.0x at 245 days (35 weeks).

**Q: When mainnet?**
A: After external security audit (8-13 weeks estimated). Testnet deployment imminent.

**Q: Can I change factions?**
A: Change anytime UNTIL you play your first game of an epoch. Then you're locked for those 4 days.
